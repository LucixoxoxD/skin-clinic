Common questions

Straight answers for Indian skin.

Everything you want to know before your first visit — answered honestly.

Is laser safe for dusky or wheatish Indian skin? +
Yes, with the right wavelength and settings. We use 810nm diode and long-pulse Nd:YAG — both gold-standard for Fitzpatrick III–V Indian skin tones. We always do a patch test first and start conservative. Results improve gradually and safely.
How many sessions will I need? +
Acne: typically 4–6 weeks to see a significant difference. Pigmentation/melasma: 3–6 months (melasma is chronic, we manage it long-term). Acne scars: 3–5 RF microneedling sessions spaced 6 weeks apart. We give you a written timeline on day one.
Do you force packages? +
Never. You pay per session. We may recommend a treatment plan for the best clinical outcome, but the choice is entirely yours. No pressure tactics, no guilt trips. Our reputation is built on honest recommendations.
Will my pigmentation come back after treatment? +
Melasma can recur with sun exposure, heat, and hormonal changes — this is a chronic condition. We teach proper daily sunscreen use (yes, even for brown skin indoors) and provide maintenance peels every 8–12 weeks in summer and monsoon season.
Is there any downtime after treatments? +
HydraFacial: zero downtime. Chemical peels: 2–3 days of mild flaking. RF microneedling: 24–48 hours of mild pinkness. We plan all treatments around your weddings, events, and travel schedule so you're never caught off guard.
Do you treat teenagers and young adults? +
Yes, we see patients from 14 years onwards. For those under 18, a parent is required for the first consultation. Our approach focuses on barrier repair and gentle evidence-based actives — no harsh scrubs, no steroids.
